## Our Commitment

AegisPHP is committed to fostering an open, respectful, and professional
environment for everyone. We value constructive discussion, technical rigor,
and mutual respect.

Participation in this project means agreeing to uphold this Code of Conduct.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Respectful and professional communication
- Constructive technical feedback
- Openness to differing viewpoints
- Clear, well-reasoned architectural discussions
- Supporting newcomers and contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Demeaning language or insults
- Public shaming
- Trolling or disruptive behavior
- Sharing private information without consent

---

## Technical Disagreements

Strong technical opinions are welcome.
Disrespect is not.

When disagreeing:

- Focus on the architecture or implementation, not the person
- Provide reasoning and evidence
- Propose alternatives constructively
- Keep discussions aligned with project principles

AegisPHP values architectural discipline and security-first thinking,
but never at the expense of professionalism.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Actions may include:

- Requesting clarification
- Removing inappropriate comments
- Restricting or banning contributors in severe cases

Decisions are made with fairness and proportionality.
